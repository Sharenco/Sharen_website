THE SCOUT

Jeremy Willis, hailing from Boston, Massachusetts, is the sprightly offensive class of the team. With unmatched speed and agility, he navigates the battlefield with acrobatic grace through his double jump. His youthful bravado makes him both an asset and a liability. Always bold, often reckless!

With some good (or bad) luck you can find these players using this merc:
- Wallywest
- Bootywarrior
- Alvarez
- Princevontwix
- Rals
